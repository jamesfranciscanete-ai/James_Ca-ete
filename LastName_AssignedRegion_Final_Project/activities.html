Activities & Experiences

Adventure & Outdoors

  • Summiting the trails in the Cascade Mountains.
  • Kayaking or sailing the Puget Sound waters.
  • Mountain biking through dense, damp rainforests.

Culture & Art

  • Visiting the numerous museums in Seattle (e.g., MoPOP).
  • Exploring the historic Pike Place Market in Seattle.
  • Discovering the unique art galleries of Portland.

Food & Drink

  • Sampling world-famous craft coffee and microbrews.
  • Eating fresh, locally-caught Pacific Salmon and Oysters.
  • Experiencing Portland's famous food cart pods.
