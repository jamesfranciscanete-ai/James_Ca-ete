Travel Tips

  • **Pack layers:** Weather changes quickly from coast to mountains.
  • Book ferry and state park passes well in advance, especially in peak season.
  • Allow extra time for travel; city traffic can be heavy.
  • The PNW is beautiful year-round, but summer offers the best hiking and driest weather.

Basic Packing Checklist

  • Waterproof/Rain Jacket
  • Hiking Boots or sturdy, comfortable walking shoes
  • Reusable water bottle and coffee mug
  • Comfortable layers (fleece/sweaters)
  • Camera for stunning views
